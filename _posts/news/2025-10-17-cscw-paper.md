Hallo! Thrilled to be in Norway to present my new paper at *ACM CSCW:* ["Beyond Big Tech: Advocacy Technologists within Mission-Driven Civil Society Organizations"](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3757559). (I've also put together a friendly [one-page infographic](pdfs/Chambers_CSCW_2025_1p.pdf) about it.) 
<!--more-->
This solo-author work seeks to characterize a new class of professionals that I term _advocacy technologists:_ tech experts operating within nonprofit and civil society organizations.
