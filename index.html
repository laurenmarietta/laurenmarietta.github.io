Hello!

I am currently working as a Research and Instrument Analyst for the James Webb Space Telescope mission at Space Telescope Science Institute in Baltimore, Maryland. Originally from Hampton, VA, I recieved my B.S. from Yale in 2017, where I double-majored in astrophysics and African American studies. My primary academic interests include astrochemistry, gravitational wave astrophysics, and science studies. Additionally, I have a growing interest in applying quantitative analytic methods to social problems.

I am immensely passionate about working towards equity and inclusion in all of the spaces that I occupy.
