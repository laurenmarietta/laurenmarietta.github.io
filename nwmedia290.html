My fellow Americans:

Today, this nation is transformed. Democracy has won. The citizens of this nation have won.

Today, we celebrate the triumph not of a candidate, nor of an individual, but of a transformation: the digitization of democracy.

The past centuries have shown how democracy is both precious and fragile. And at this hour, my friends, democracy will never again be threatened.

With this historic election, our country is taking a brave step into the post-analog future. Gone are the days of bias, mistakes, accidents, and uncertainty. My administration will set us on a path of progress, unity, and certitude.

Americans have chosen truth and security over division and gridlock.

From this day forth, history will be permanently recorded in not just books, but in code.

We are finally living out the promise of the Digital Age.

American innovation has changed the world for centuries: first with our Constitution, then with our economy, and later our global dominance. Today, we celebrate this latest triumph of American innovation, from sea to shining sea, from the birthplace of the computer in Silicon Valley to the seat of our government in Washington, D.C.

The rest of the world is watching, some doubtful of what my administration will mean for them. Yet they will soon see, as the brave American people have, that the era of human governance has given way to the era of algorithmic governance.

As your chosen leader, I will rule with a silicon fist to protect American democracy and the American dream.

There need be no doubt that I will be a President for all Americans. It is written as the most fundamental rule of my programming.

Many of you understandably have questions. Rest assured that transparency is one primary protocols. In this eAddress, I present my plans for what this administration will bring....
